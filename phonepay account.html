How to do mobile recharge -

Follow these steps -

  1. Open the Paytm mobile application
  2. Click on ‘Recharge & Pay Bills’
  3. Click on ‘Mobile Recharge’
  4. Choose Prepaid
  5. Enter Mobile Number
  6. Check Plans
  7. Click on Proceed/Pay Button
  8. Choose the method to pay and click pay button
