How to do Payments using Credit/Debit Card

Follow these Following steps :-



Step 1.Select Credit/Debit Card to pay with your Cards.




Step 2.Enter the Card Details.




Step 3.After Clicking on Pay,It will redirect to bank payment page and you will receive OTP on mobile number linked with the Card.Enter OTP and click on "Make Payment."




Step 4.Congrats, the payments is done .
