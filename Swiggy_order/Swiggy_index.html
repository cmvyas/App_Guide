How to Order food from Swiggy -

Follow these steps -

  1. Open the Swiggy mobile application
  2. Click on ‘Restaurants From where you want to order food’
  3. Click on ‘Find Offers also’
  4. Click on ‘View Cart’
  5. Choose the Payment Option
  6. Click on ‘Place Order’
  7. Give Feedback
