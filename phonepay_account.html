How to create account on phonepay -

Follow these steps -

  1. Download the app from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
  2. After downloading the app, open the app and verify your mobile number. (Be sure that the mobile number you entered should be the same one registered with your bank account).
  3. Enter all the required details (Name, email address and set your 4-digit password and activate your wallet.
  4. Tap on creating new Virtual Private Address(VPA).
  5. Select your bank account to link, the app will automatically fetch the details.
  6. Confirm your bank details.
