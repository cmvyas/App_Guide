How to book bus ticket on redBus app

Follow these steps -

  1. Open the official website of redBus as mentioned link Open or open a redBus mobile application
  2. Enter the Source and Destination locations in the 'From' and 'To' tab respectively
  3. Enter the 'Date of Journey' and hit the 'Search' button
  4. Select a bus of your choice from the list of buses that are displayed
  5. Select a seat, boarding, and dropping points and hit 'Proceed to Book'
  6. Key in the 'Passenger Information' and 'Contact Information'
  7. Put the Details
  8. You can even choose to insure your journey so that you will be covered in case of an accident or loss of luggage
  9. Check the summary and Enter the Captcha click Proceed to Pay
  10. Enter the offer code (if applicable), select your mode of payment and complete the payment procedure
  11. Click the Proceed Button
  12. Once this is done, you will receive an M-ticket on your mobile and an e-ticket on the email IDthat was entered under 'Contact Information'.
