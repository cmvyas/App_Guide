How to Pay for your Water Bills -

On the Application-

  1. Open Paytm App
  2. Click on ‘Recharge & Pay Bills’
  3. Click on ‘Water’
  4. Select your water supply board
  5. Select your service type (if any)
  6. Click on ‘Proceed’ to check the water bill amount and pay
