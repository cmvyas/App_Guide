How to book bus ticket using paytm -

Follow these steps -

  1. Open the Paytm mobile application
  2. Click on "Bus Tickets"
  3. Tap on "From" and enter the city you are travelling from
  4. Tap on "To Where" and enter the city you are travelling to
  5. Tap on "Departure Date" and from the calendar select your departure date
  6. Tap on "Search Buses" button
  7. From the list of options choose your suitable bus based on timings and price
  8. Choose the Seats: From the displayed seat map you can choose the seat according to your preference. Seats shown in white are available whereas the grey seats are already occupied
  9. Now tap on the "Proceed" option at the bottom right corner of the page. The selected seat will be highlighted in green Color
  10. Check the circle check box to select the preferred boarding and drop point
  11. Tap "Proceed"
  12. Enter the full name, age, contact information of the traveler in the respective boxes
  13. Review your booking and tap "Proceed"
  14. Tap on the "Yes I understand. Proceed to Pay"
  15. Tap on Pay Securely
  16. After your payment is successful you will get a Booked status on your booking.You will get your ticket on email and SMS or you can download from here also
